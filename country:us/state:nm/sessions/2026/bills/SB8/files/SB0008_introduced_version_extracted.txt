SB0008 SENATE BILL 8 57th legislature - STATE OF NEW MEXICO - second session, 2026 INTRODUCED BY Antoinette Sedillo Lopez and Harold Pope and George K. Muñoz and Pete Campos and Shannon D. Pinto AN ACT AUTHORIZING A TRANSFER FROM THE GENERAL FUND TO THE BEHAVIORAL HEALTH TRUST FUND. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO: SECTION 1. GENERAL FUND TRANSFER.--Six hundred fifty million dollars ($650,000,000) is transferred from the general fund to the behavioral health trust fund in fiscal year 2027. .232842.1
