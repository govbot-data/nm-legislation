10 AN ACT
11 MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE BOARD OF REGENTS OF THE

15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO:
16 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION.--Three million dollars
17 ($3,000,000) is appropriated from the general fund to the board
18 of regents of the university of New Mexico for expenditure in
19 fiscal years 2027 through 2029 for the university of New
20 Mexico's graduate medical education program to assist hospitals
21 in the state to develop and expand physician residencies in
22 family practice, internal medicine, obstetrics, gynecology and
23 pediatrics in medically underserved rural areas of the state.
24 Any unexpended balance remaining at the end of fiscal year 2029
25 shall revert to the general fund.
