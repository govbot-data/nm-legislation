SECTION 1. PUBLIC SAFETY WORKFORCE BUILDING PROGRAM--
18
19 GRANTS.--
A. The "public safety workforce building program"
20
is created in the department to provide grant funding for
21

22

23

3 B. To receive a grant, an applicant shall apply to
4 the department on a form and in a manner prescribed by the
5 department. An application shall include:
6 (1) a description of the initiative proposed
7 by the applicant;
8 (2) a description of the initiative that
9 demonstrates it will meet an objective of the program; and
10 (3) details and specifics as to the
11 reasonable expected costs and completion date of the proposed
12 initiative.
13 C. The department shall determine which proposed
14 initiatives are approved to receive a grant through a
15 competitive review process pursuant to rules promulgated by
16 the department. The department may adjust the amount of
17 money requested for a proposed initiative based on its
determination of the reasonable costs of the initiative.
18
Once an initiative is approved, the department shall promptly
19
offer each applicant a grant, as appropriate. If a grantee
20
accepts an offer, the initiative may commence, and the
21
grantee shall report to the department on the progress of the

23
department, including an accounting of expenditures made for
24
the initiative. Funds allocated to an approved initiative

6 fund.
7 D. The department shall develop criteria to guide

9 (1) take into consideration:

SECTION 2. PUBLIC SAFETY WORKFORCE CAPACITY BUILDING
22
FUND--CREATED.--The "public safety workforce capacity
23
building fund" is created as a nonreverting fund in the state
24
treasury to support the objectives of the public safety
25 HB 255
Page 7

1 workforce building program. The fund consists of
2 distributions, appropriations, gifts, grants, donations and
3 income from investment of the fund. Money in the fund is
4 appropriated to the department of finance and administration.
5 Expenditures from the fund shall be made on warrant of the
6 secretary of finance and administration pursuant to vouchers
7 signed by the secretary of finance and administration or the
8 secretary's authorized representative. The department of
9 finance and administration shall administer the fund to
10 provide grant disbursements and pay administrative costs
11 necessary to carry out the provisions of Section 1 of this
12 2026 act; provided that the amount expended for
13 administrative costs shall not exceed three percent of the
14 annual balance of the fund.
