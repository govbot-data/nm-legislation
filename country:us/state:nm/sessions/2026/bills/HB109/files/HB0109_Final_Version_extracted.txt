9 SECTION 1. A new section of the Water Project Finance

11 "PROJECT PRIORITIZATION.--
12 A. The board shall prioritize projects based on
13 the following criteria:
14 (1) urgency of need, as identified in a
15 regional water planning area with a completed regional water
16 plan accepted by the interstate stream commission;
17 (2) availability of federal or local
18 matching contributions;
19 (3) project readiness, including the
acquisition of required state and federal permits and
20
authorizations;
21
(4) regional impacts;
22
(5) efforts to mitigate or alleviate a
23
project's potential human health and safety concerns;
24
(6) level of planning and design;

1 (7) improvement of water quality or
2 quantity; and
3 (8) other goals established for each project
4 type pursuant to the Water Project Finance Act and rules
5 adopted under that act.
6 B. Pursuant to Subsection A of this section, the
7 board shall give higher priority to projects that:
8 (1) have completed planning and design;
9 (2) provide regional impacts;
10 (3) mitigate or alleviate human health and
11 safety concerns; and
12 (4) improve water quality or quantity and
13 advance project-type specific goals established pursuant to
14 the Water Project Finance Act.
15 C. The board shall develop and use a weighted
16 scoring system that incorporates the criteria provided
17 pursuant to Subsection A of this section to evaluate and rank
all applications for loans and grants pursuant to the Water
18
Project Finance Act. A ten-point score increase shall be
19
granted in the event of an emergency situation.
20

22
