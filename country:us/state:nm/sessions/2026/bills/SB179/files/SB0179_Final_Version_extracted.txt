16 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION.--Three hundred fifty thousand
17 dollars ($350,000) is appropriated from the general fund to the
18 board of regents of the university of New Mexico for
19 expenditure in fiscal years 2027 and 2028 for the health
20 sciences center office for diversity, equity and inclusion for
21 Spanish-language health sciences curriculum development and
22 implementation to enhance the medical Spanish education program
